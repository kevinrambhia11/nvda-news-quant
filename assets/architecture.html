NVDA news-quant desk · system architecture · 2026-07-22

News goes in. A position size comes out.
Everything in between has to earn trust.

The desk reads global news and NVDA prices every day, turns them into a position size with hard risk limits (its one trusted product), plus advisory context it deliberately does not trust. Its core finding, tested nine ways: news predicts how BIG tomorrow's move will be — not which direction.

STAGE 4THE TOURNAMENT — where trust is earned

Nothing gets promoted by argument. Candidate models compete on a judging window their feature-builders never saw, and the winner's real grade comes from a holdout that nothing was ever tuned on. Every Saturday, every seat is re-contested.

VOLATILITY — how big is tomorrow?

PRODUCT

HAR + earnings events. Holdout R² 0.31 (1-day) / 0.42 (5-day). News vectors challenged it for the seat — and lost. This model alone sizes real risk.

MAGNITUDE — bigger than normal?

ON PROBATION

P(move > its trailing median). News lifts holdout AUC 0.537 → 0.567, calibrated so the probabilities mean what they say. Published daily; graded live on the Track record tab.

DIRECTION — up or down?

ADVISORY

Nine architectures, none beat buy-and-hold on the holdout — consistent with an efficient market. Shown for context, never trusted with money.

How the timeline keeps everyone honest: models may learn from the past on their left only. News scorers train on scorer training years; every candidate is compared on the clean judging window those scorers never saw; the crowned winner's real grade comes from the untouched holdout — and the boundaries are pinned dates, so weekly retrains can never quietly nibble at them. Live is the only test that can't be gamed at all: forecasts published before the open, scored after.

The verdict ledger — what was tested, what survived

Every hypothesis met the same judge: compete on days nothing was tuned on. Most lost. That is the point.

HypothesisEvidenceVerdict
Volatility is predictable HAR + events beats every benchmark and every challenger, news included; holdout R² 0.31 / 0.42 YES — the product
News predicts move size Honest windows: holdout AUC 0.537 → 0.567 with news2 vectors; calibrated Brier 0.250 ON PROBATION — strongest news result
News predicts direction Nine architectures over four toolchains; best holdout still under buy-and-hold; news2 direction AUC 0.466 NO — advisory only
A neural net reads news better than ridge NewsNet's early edge traced to a data bug (98% of articles paired with wrong embeddings); on fixed inputs it adds nothing NO — artifact of the bug
News improves the vol forecast (sizing) News-vector candidates mid-pack at both horizons on the clean window NO
Premium sources (Reuters/Bloomberg/FT) predict direction Dead last of the field — vetted coverage is priced fastest NO
Competitor / industry news predicts direction Mid-pack, no holdout edge NO
Minutes-scale news trading on free feeds Event study: price fully moved before the 15-min feed even updated NO-GO
